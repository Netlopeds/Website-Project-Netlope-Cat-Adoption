Fostering saves lives!

Thank you for considering fostering. When you foster a cat, you are saving them from a life on the streets, confined existence in a cage, or unnecessary euthanasia. At Tenth Life, we are seeking true forever homes. Most of our cats live in foster homes while they await adoption which is how we know so much about their habits, likes, and dislikes. Our adoption counselors are happy to help you every step of the way to find the best match for you.
